G. Ceren (Gerry) Aksu

Hi! I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Marketing at Rutgers Business School. My research interests fall broadly in the domain of understanding consumer responses to various brand actions. As such, my work examines the joint influence of brand-level factors and brand behaviors on consumer behavior and decision making.
