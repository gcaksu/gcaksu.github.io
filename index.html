G. Ceren (Gerry) Aksu

Hi! I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Marketing at Rutgers Business School.

My research interests lie in the areas of branding and social media. I became interested in these topics during my MBA program at Pace University. I was fascinated by how consumers respond to brands and how they participate in social media. I am thrilled to be pursuing these interests in my PhD program. I follow an experimental, consumer behavior approach and advance our understanding of how consumers respond to various branding actions.

Aside from being a PhD student, I am also Rutgers Business School (RBS) Behavioral Lab coordinator. In this lab, we conduct research studies in Marketing, Management and Accounting for faculty and PhD students.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring new places, listening to traditional folk music from around the world, and gardening.
